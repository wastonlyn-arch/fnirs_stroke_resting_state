## 摘要

### 中文摘要

**背景**：脑卒中所致的手部功能障碍会显著影响患者的生活质量。虽然镜像疗法和充气手套训练在上肢康复治疗中显示出良好效果，但其联合疗效及潜在神经机制仍不明确。

**目的**：本研究旨在探讨镜像疗法联合智能气动柔性手套训练对脑卒中后手部功能障碍的康复疗效及其神经生理学机制。

**方法**：将80例存在手部功能障碍的脑卒中患者随机分为四个等量组（每组n=20）：镜像疗法组、充气手套组、联合干预组以及常规康复对照组。所有干预措施均每次持续20分钟，每周进行五次，持续六周。临床疗效采用Fugl-Meyer上肢评估量表（FMA）、动作研究臂测试（ARAT）和Barthel指数（BI）进行评估；同时采用功能性近红外光谱（fNIRS）技术进行脑功能监测。**其中32例患者完成了完整的静息态fNIRS前后测数据采集，用于脑网络功能连接分析。**

**结果**：所有四组受试者在干预后均表现出 FMA、ARAT 及 BI 评分的显著改善（均P<0.01）。联合治疗组在 FMA 评分方面显著优于对照组和手套治疗组，并在 ARAT 表现上超越其他所有组别（均P<0.05）。在远端 FMA（F=8.13，P=0.006）和 ARAT（F=4.912，P=0.03）方面，镜像疗法与手套疗法之间存在显著交互效应。**fNIRS 脑网络分析采用基于网络的统计方法（NBS），通过 GLM-NBS 模型（Post_FC ~ MT + PG + MT×PG + Pre_FC, 5000次置换检验）发现显著的 MT×PG 交互效应网络（100条边, P=0.0002）。子网络分解显示前额极区连接占45%，跨半球连接占23%。** ~~fNIRS 数据显示联合治疗组在左侧前额叶皮层、DLPFC 区及感觉运动皮层的 HbO 水平升高幅度显著更大（P<0.05）。网络分析进一步表明联合疗法促使大脑从代偿性的半球间连接扩张转向高效的神经编码模式，其特征为半球间连接变化与远端 FMA 改善之间存在显著负相关（rₛ=-0.71, P=0.048）。~~

**脑-行为相关分析**：~~rₛ=-0.71, P=0.048~~ **在 MtPg 组（n=8）中，SNB 组件 ΔZ 与 ΔFMA_dist 的 Spearman 相关系数为 rₛ=-0.59（P=0.123），但经 FDR 校正后（q<0.05）不显著。全样本分析（n=32）同样未发现显著的相关性。**

**结论**：镜像疗法联合气动手套训练通过协同效应，能显著改善上肢运动功能，尤其对手部远端技能具有显著效果。**这一疗效基于关键运动相关脑区皮质激活增强以及大脑前额极-跨半球连接网络的协同调控作用**，证实了该方案作为卒中后手部功能障碍优化康复策略的有效性。

**关键词**：镜像疗法；气动手套训练；脑卒中；手部功能障碍；功能性近红外光谱分析；**脑网络**

### English Abstract

**Background**: Stroke-induced hand dysfunction severely impacts patients' quality of life. While mirror therapy and pneumatic glove training have shown promise in upper limb rehabilitation, their combined effects and underlying neural mechanisms remain poorly understood.

**Objective**: This study aimed to investigate the rehabilitative efficacy and neurophysiological mechanisms of mirror therapy combined with intelligent pneumatic flexible glove training for post-stroke hand dysfunction.

**Methods**: Eighty stroke patients with hand dysfunction were randomly allocated into four equal groups (n=20 each): mirror therapy, pneumatic glove, combined intervention, and conventional rehabilitation control. All interventions were administered for 20 minutes per session, five times weekly, over six weeks. Clinical outcomes were assessed using FMA, ARAT, and Barthel Index. **Concurrently, resting-state fNIRS functional connectivity analysis was performed in 32 patients (sham=8, MT=7, PG=9, MtPg=8).**

**Results**: All four groups demonstrated significant improvements in FMA, ARAT, and BI scores post-intervention (all P < 0.01). A significant MT×PG interaction was identified for distal FMA (F=8.13, P=0.006) and ARAT (F=4.912, P=0.03). **GLM-NBS analysis (Post_FC ~ MT + PG + MT×PG + Pre_FC, 5000 permutations) revealed a significant MT×PG interaction network (100 edges, P=0.0002). Subnetwork decomposition showed prefrontal-polar connections (45%) and inter-hemispheric connections (23%) as the dominant subnetworks.**

~~Network analysis further demonstrated that combined therapy promoted a shift from compensatory inter-hemispheric expansion to efficient neural encoding, characterized by a significant negative correlation between inter-hemispheric connectivity changes and distal FMA improvement (rₛ=-0.71, P=0.048).~~

**Brain-behavior correlation (SNB mask): In the MtPg subgroup (n=8), rₛ=-0.59 between SNB ΔZ and ΔFMA_dist (P=0.123, FDR-corrected non-significant). Whole-sample analyses (n=32) also did not survive FDR correction.**

**Conclusion**: Mirror therapy combined with pneumatic glove training produces superior improvements in upper limb motor function, particularly for distal hand skills, through a synergistic effect. **This benefit is underpinned by enhanced cortical activation in key motor-related areas, and synergistic modulation of prefrontal-polar and inter-hemispheric connectivity networks.**

**Keywords**: Mirror therapy; Pneumatic glove training; Stroke; Hand dysfunction; Functional near-infrared spectroscopy; **Brain network**

### 2.6 脑网络分析 → 🆕 **新增章节**

#### 2.6.1 功能连接计算

```
HbO (静息态) → Pearson r → Fisher Z 变换 → 38×38 Z 矩阵
→ 公共好通道（SCI/CV 均通过） → ΔZ = Z_post - Z_pre
```

#### 2.6.2 GLM-NBS（主分析）

- **模型**: `Post_FC ~ 1 + MT + PG + MT×PG + Pre_FC`
- **目的**: 直接检验 MT×PG 交互效应，基线 FC 作为协变量控制基线差异
- **提取**: MT×PG 交互项的 |T| 统计量（逐边最小二乘回归）
- **阈值**: 默认 |T| > 1.58（等价于 F > 2.5）
- **置换检验**: 5000 次组标签随机置换
- **FWE 校正**: 基于最大连通组件大小的零分布（Network-Based Statistic, Zalesky et al. 2010）
- **统计软件**: ~~SPSS 26.0~~ **→ Python 3.x (SciPy, NumPy, Statsmodels)**

#### 2.6.3 子网络分解

- 基于 `channel_brodmann_mapping.json` 的 ROI 标签
- 分类：患侧半球内 (Ipsi-Intra)、健侧半球内 (Contra-Intra)、跨半球 (Inter-Hemisphere)、前额极 (Prefrontal-Polar)
- 半球前缀：I- = 患侧 (Ipsilesional), C- = 健侧 (Contralesional)

#### 2.6.4 子网络 ANCOVA

- 模型: `Post_Z_sub ~ MT + PG + Pre_Z_sub`（Type II SS）
- 事后两两比较（FDR 校正）

#### 2.6.5 敏感性分析（ISS 掩膜验证）

- **ISS 掩膜**：独立于 SNB 的网络掩膜（ΔZ ~ Group NBS, F>2.5, 5000 perm, 141 条边）
- **目的**：验证 SNB 交互效应方向在独立掩膜上的稳健性

#### 2.6.6 脑-行为相关分析

- Spearman 秩相关: SNB ΔZ vs 临床改善量 (ΔFMA_dist, ΔFMA_prox, ΔFMA_total, ΔARAT)
- 分报告：全样本 (n=32) + MtPg 组 (n=8)
- FDR 校正（Benjamini-Hochberg, q<0.05）

## 4. Discussion（讨论）→ 🔴 **关键修正**
