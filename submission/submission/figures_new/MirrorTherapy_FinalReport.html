Network Discovery Text:

To identify the functional connectivity network significantly modulated by the interaction between Mirror Therapy (MT) and Pneumatic Glove (PG), we fitted an edge-wise ANCOVA model: $\\text{Post\\_FC} \\sim \\text{MT} \\times \\text{PG} + \\text{Pre\\_FC}$ (Type III SS). The interaction $t$-statistics were subjected to Network-Based Statistics (NBS) with 5000 permutations, a primary threshold of $|t| > 1.58$, and graph-theoretic connectivity (shared node topology). One significant component was identified — the Synergistic Network Block (SNB) — comprising $100$ edges across $37$ nodes, with a family-wise error (FWE) corrected $p = 0.0002$. This component indicates that the combined MT+PG intervention induces a non-additive reorganization of inter-hemispheric functional connectivity beyond the sum of individual effects.
